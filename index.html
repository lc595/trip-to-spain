My Favorite Places

Park Guell

Park Guell is an expansive park located in Barcelona. The views are absolutely gorgeous overlooking the water and the city. The architect, Antoni Gaudi, is also responsible for the extravagent Sagrada Familia cathedral. There are so many beautiful buildings in this park. Gaudi, himself even lived in one long ago. Not only is the architecture beautiful around this park, but there are also so many beautiful landscapes. There is so much greenery and beautiful flowers all around. I LOVE THIS PLACE!

Park Guell is a beautiful outdoor landscape designed by the famous architect, Antoni Gaudi

Barceloneta Beach

Barceloneta beach is a gorgeous beach. There are so many delicious restaurants along the water and so many fun activities. You can see people rollerblading, playing beach volleyball, or swimming in the ocean! One of my favorite restaurants here is called Can Majo. The paella there is TO DIE FOR! The paella is filled with yummy rice, calamari, prawns, mussels and clams. So delicious.

Barceloneta Beach is surrounded by palm trees and yummy eateries
